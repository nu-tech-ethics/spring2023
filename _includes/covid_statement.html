Learning During COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic is still ongoing and will present new, unexpected challenges to our class and to our community at Northwestern. I want to acknowledge that things are difficult for many of us on multiple fronts. Some thoughts:

  1. Your health and well-being is always top priority. Please communicate with me (Sarah) or a member of the course staff if you have something going on so that we can figure out a path forward together.
  2. There are numerous resources at Northwestern that are in place to support students with extenuating circumstances (e.g. CAPS, talking to your academic advisor, talking to the dean of students, etc.). I can help you navigate these paths if you need help.
